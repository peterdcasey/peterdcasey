Circuit Design

Connecting Pins

The basic idea is simply to drag from one pin to the next. Releasing the
mouse and pressing again allows corners or continuing. Clicking and dragging
on an existing wire allows a wire to branch.

Four Bit Adder

The top eight pins are two 4 bit inputs, the bottom four are output.
There is also a carry in and a carry out. The two four bit inputs
represent two binary numbers. The numbers show each bit postion's
value. Dark green is zero, bright green is one.
